## 💡 ContentPulse Vision

### The Problem
Content creators rely on gut feeling instead of data.

### The Solution
AI-powered platform that:
- 🔗 Connects to YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn
- 📊 Accepts analytics reports (CSV, Excel, PDF)
- 🤖 Analyzes with Google Gemini AI
- 📈 Provides actionable insights
- ✅ Recommends what to continue, stop, and create

### The Impact
**Transform data into action. Data-driven content strategy for everyone.**
